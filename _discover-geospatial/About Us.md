<p>The Singapore Land Authority (SLA) plays a vital role as the national
geospatial agency, serving as the guardian of Singapore's national spatial
data infrastructure. As the national authority for land survey, SLA also
maintains the geodetic survey system and ensures the accuracy of land boundaries
across Singapore, providing the fundamental framework for all property
boundaries and construction works.</p>
<p></p>
<p>At the heart of our responsibilities is the facilitating, sharing and
integration of geospatial data across government agencies, enabling better
coordination and decision-making. SLA also oversees the development and
maintenance of OneMap, Singapore's authoritative national map platform
that provides reliable, up-to-date geospatial information to both public
and private sectors.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Beyond its technical functions, we drive innovation in the geospatial
industry by promoting the adoption of new technologies and standards, through
collaboration with various stakeholders to develop geospatial solutions
that support Singapore's Smart Nation initiative, from 3D mapping of the
city to the implementation of advanced surveying technologies. Through
these efforts, SLA helps ensure that Singapore's geospatial capabilities
remain at the forefront of technological advancement, supporting the nation's
development and growth.</p>
