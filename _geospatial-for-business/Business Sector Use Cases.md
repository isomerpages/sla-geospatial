<p>Deriving geospatial information through various SLA geospatial platforms
such as OneMap and SiReNT (Singapore Satellite Positioning Reference Network)
has become an integral part of various industries in Singapore, transforming
how businesses operate and serve their customers. For example, In the real
estate sector, property agencies and developers harness OneMap APIs to
provide potential buyers with detailed location insights, including nearby
amenities, transportation links, and demographic data of neighbourhoods.
Transport and logistics companies leverage OneMap's routing capabilities
to optimise delivery routes, track fleet movements to improve operational
efficiency and service reliability. The ability to integrate address verification
and postal code validation has also significantly reduced delivery errors
and improved last-mile delivery success rates.</p>

<p>For the construction and autonomous vehicles industries, SiReNT provides
crucial high-precision positioning data that enables centimetre-level accuracy
for surveying, Building Information Modelling (BIM), and precise positioning
of structural elements, which is especially valuable in Singapore's dense
urban environment. It also delivers Real-Time Kinematic (RTK) corrections
that allow exact positioning on roads, essential for safe navigation and
testing of autonomous vehicles in Singapore.</p>
