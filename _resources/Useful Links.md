<p><strong>United Nations Committee of Experts on Global Geospatial Information Management</strong>
</p>
<p>The United Nations Committee of Experts on Global Geospatial Information
Management (UN-GGIM) is a formal intergovernmental mechanism established
by the UN Economic and Social Council in 2011 to enhance global collaboration
in geospatial information management. Through its work on the Integrated
Geospatial Information Framework (IGIF) and various initiatives, the committee
guides nations in developing their geospatial capabilities, addresses global
challenges like sustainable development and climate change, and facilitates
joint decision-making on geospatial information use within national, regional
and global policy frameworks.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Visit <a href="https://ggim.un.org/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://ggim.un.org/</a> for
more information</p>

<p><strong>Open Geospatial Consortium</strong>
</p>
<p>The Open Geospatial Consortium (OGC) is an international voluntary consensus
standards organization that develops and maintains international standards
for geospatial content and location-based services, sensor web, Internet
of Things, GIS data processing and data sharing.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Visit <a href="https://www.ogc.org/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.ogc.org/</a> for
more information</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>ASEAN Geospatial Resource Portal</strong>
</p>
<p>The ASEAN Geospatial Portal is a collaborative effort by the ASEAN Geospatial
Agency Members to offer a valuable resource for individuals and organisations
seeking to delve into the dynamic landscape of geospatial technologies
and initiatives within Southeast Asia. The portal seeks to enable users
to gain an in-depth understanding of ASEAN's geospatial landscape, find
geospatial educational opportunities and stay abreast on key industry events.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Visit <a href="https://ggim.un.org/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.aseangeospatial.org/</a> for
more information</p>
