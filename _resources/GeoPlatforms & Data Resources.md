<h2>OneMap</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 40%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/OneMap.png">
</div>
<p><a href="https://www.onemap.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">OneMap</a> is
the national map platform to enable government agencies to deliver geospatial
information and services to the general public.</p>
<p>Launched in 2010, OneMap is built on open-sourced platforms, offers location-based
information and navigational services, as well as Application Programming
Interface (API) for application developers.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/OM.png">
</div>
<p>The OneMap platform offers:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Point of Interest Location and information (POIs) including</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>State Properties</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Local Amenities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Healthcare</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Schools</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Land Lot and Land Ownership Information</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Residential Property Information Including</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Type of Residential Property</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Property Price&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Transaction Period</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Airspace Zoning&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Multi-Modal Routing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Multi-Stop Routing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Distance and ETA Estimations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Real Time Traffic Information</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><a href="https://www.onemap.gov.sg/apidocs/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">OneMap APIs</a> are
available for:&nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Base Maps</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Address Search</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Coordinate Converter</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Reverse Geocode</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Thematic Information</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Planning Area</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demographic Data</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Routing&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Advanced Mini-map</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Static Map</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h2>INLIS</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/INLIS.png">
</div>
<p><a href="https://app.sla.gov.sg/inlis/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Integrated Land Information Service</u></a>&nbsp;is
a one-stop online portal which allows users to search and purchase land
and property-related information such as property ownership, survey plans
and road line plans.</p>
