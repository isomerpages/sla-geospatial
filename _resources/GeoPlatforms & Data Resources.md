<h2>OneMap</h2><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 40%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/OneMap.png"></div><p><a href="https://www.onemap.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">OneMap</a> is the national map platform to enable government agencies to deliver geospatial information and services to the people, public and private sectors.</p><p>Launched in 2010, OneMap is built on open-sourced platforms, offers location-based information and navigational services, as well as Application Programming Interface (API) for application developers.</p><p>The OneMap platform offers:</p><ul><li><p>Point of Interest Location and information (POIs) including</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>State Properties</p></li><li><p>Parks</p></li><li><p>Local Amenities</p></li><li><p>Healthcare</p></li><li><p>Schools</p></li></ul></li><li><p>Land Lot and Land Ownership Information</p></li><li><p>Residential Property Information Including</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Type of Residential Property</p></li><li><p>Property Price&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Transaction Period</p></li></ul></li><li><p>Airspace Zoning&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Multi-Modal Routing</p></li><li><p>Multi-Stop Routing</p></li><li><p>Distance and ETA Estimations</p></li><li><p>Real Time Traffic Information</p></li></ul><p><a href="https://www.onemap.gov.sg/apidocs/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">OneMap APIs</a> are available for:&nbsp;</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Base Maps</p></li><li><p>Address Search</p></li><li><p>Coordinate Converter</p></li><li><p>Reverse Geocode</p></li><li><p>Thematic Information</p></li><li><p>Planning Area</p></li><li><p>Demographic Data</p></li><li><p>Routing&nbsp;</p></li><li><p>Advanced Mini-map</p></li><li><p>Static Map</p></li></ul><hr><h2>Licensed Data</h2><p>The Singapore Land Authority (SLA) is the trusted centre for authoritative government geospatial data in Singapore.&nbsp;</p><p>SLA offers geospatial data that are authoritative, accurate, granular, timely and localised via SLA products that include Licensed Data.&nbsp;</p><p>Licensed Data can be used in sectors such as transport and logistics for real time fleet monitoring.&nbsp;</p><p>The types of datasets available under SLA Licensed Data are:&nbsp;</p><p>• SLA Street Directory</p><p>• SLA Address Point</p><p>• SLA Digitised Cadastral</p><p>• Land Base Road Network</p><p>• Land Base Building Outline</p><hr><h2>INLIS</h2><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/INLIS.png"></div><p><a href="https://app.sla.gov.sg/inlis/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Integrated Land Information Service</u></a>&nbsp;is a one-stop online portal which allows users to search and purchase land and property-related information such as property ownership, survey plans and road line plans.</p><p><strong>It is for:</strong></p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Law firms</p></li><li><p>Property developers</p></li><li><p>Construction project managers</p></li><li><p>Engineers</p></li><li><p>Property agents</p></li><li><p>Surveyors</p></li></ul><p>You can access a wide variety of information from the website such as:</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Property Information</p></li><li><p>Land Survey Information</p></li><li><p>Road Line Plan &amp; Railway Protection Plan</p></li><li><p>Borehole and Utility Information</p></li></ul><p></p>
