<p>GeoWorks brings together working professionals and passionate individuals
across the geospatial landscape. Our aim is to create a vibrant community
where innovation thrives through meaningful connections. Through events
and knowledge-sharing sessions, we hope to create opportunities for potential
collaborations and knowledge exchange within the community.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our flagship event, Singapore Geospatial Day (formerly known as Singapore
Geospatial Festival), is held every October. Its primary goal is to build
strong relationships and promote ideation within the geospatial community.
Additionally, GeoWorks, under the Singapore Land Authority, serves as the
Strategic Partner of Geo Connect Asia, the region’s leading geospatial
event.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
