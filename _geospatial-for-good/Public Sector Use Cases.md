<p><strong>myResponder by SCDF</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

</div>
<p>The myResponder app, developed by the Singapore Civil Defence Force (SCDF),
is a life-saving mobile application that transforms ordinary citizens into
community first responders. This innovative platform alerts registered
users to nearby cardiac arrest cases and minor fires within their immediate
vicinity, enabling them to provide crucial assistance before emergency
services arrive. The app also allows users to report fires, medical emergencies,
and other incidents directly to SCDF.</p>
<p></p>
<p>OneMap integration plays a vital role in the app's effectiveness by providing
precise location-based services. When a cardiac arrest case is reported,
the app uses OneMap's detailed mapping capabilities to pinpoint the exact
location of the incident and identify registered responders within a 400-metre
radius. The platform also displays the locations of the nearest Automated
External Defibrillators (AEDs) for first aid responders to obtain while
getting to the person in need of help.</p>

<p><strong>OneService by MSO</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/OneService.png">
</div>
<p>The OneService app, developed by the Municipal Services Office (MSO),
serves as a unified platform for Singapore residents to report municipal
issues and book facilities. This application streamlines the process of
reporting various neighbourhood concerns - from cleanliness and maintenance
issues to pest control and infrastructure problems - without requiring
citizens to know which specific agency is responsible.</p>
<p></p>
<p>When users report an issue, OneMap's precise location services help them
accurately pin the exact location of the problem, complete with detailed
building and street-level information. The platform's mapping capabilities
also enable users to view reported cases in their vicinity, check the status
of existing reports, and access information about nearby amenities and
services.</p>
