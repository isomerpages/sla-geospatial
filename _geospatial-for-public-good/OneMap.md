<p>OneMap is Singapore's authoritative national map platform developed and
maintained by the Singapore Land Authority (SLA).</p>
<p></p>
<p>Some of its key features and purposes include:</p>
<p>•Serving as a one-stop integrated map system that combines detailed location-based
information and services from various government agencies</p>
<p>•Providing reliable, up-to-date information about Singapore's landscape,
including building locations, amenities, property information, population
demographics, and transportation routes</p>
<p>•Offering both web and mobile applications to make geospatial information
easily accessible to the public, businesses, and government agencies</p>
<p>•Supporting developers with Application Programming Interface (APIs) to&nbsp;integrate
OneMap's features into their own applications, enabling the creation of
location-based services and applications</p>
<p></p>
<p>Visit <a href="http://www.onemap.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.onemap.gov.sg</a> on
the desktop or download the OneMap mobile app in Google Play Store or Apple
App Store.</p>
